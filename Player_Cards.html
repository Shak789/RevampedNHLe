Section 2

Reading the Cards

1. Team and League: Player's team and league during the
2022-2023 season.

2. Age: Player's current age.

3. NHLe: The value of the player's points relative to the NHL
(adjusted for 82-game season).

4. Potential: Projects a player's point totals. More information
can be found on the spreadsheet.

5. Rank: Player's NHLe ranked amongst other prospects. This
is adjusted for age, so if two players have the same NHLe, the
younger player will be ranked higher.
